Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SetFindr

When will SetFindr launch?

We're planning to launch in select cities in early 2025. Waitlist members will get early access and be the first to know about our launch dates.

How does the rental process work?

Renters can browse listings, check availability, and book directly through our platform. Owners approve requests, and our secure payment system handles the transaction. After the rental period, both parties leave reviews.

Is my equipment insured during rentals?

Yes, all rentals on SetFindr include insurance coverage. We offer different tiers of protection based on the value of your equipment or property.

What fees does SetFindr charge?

SetFindr takes a small commission on each transaction - 10% from owners and 5% from renters. This covers our platform costs, payment processing, and insurance.
